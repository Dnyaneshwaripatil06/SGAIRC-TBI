Our Objectives


1. To enhance aspirations towards taking entrepreneurship as a first choice.
2. To create and sustain the entrepreneurship ecosystem.
3. To handhold potential start-ups, accelerating progress on SDG.
4. Mentor grass root innovations supporting green developments.
5. Prompt Women-led start-ups supporting ESGs

The SGIARC-TBI Foundation focuses on several key areas that are aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), such as agri-tech, food tech, edu-tech, health-tech, and clean energy. These focus areas are all important for sustainability and can be supported by green finance, such as sustainable finance, SDG mapping, green insurance and many more

1. Agri-Tech:

This involves finding solutions to improve crop yields, reduce environmental impact, manage supply chains better, ensure food safety and nutrition security, and increase profitability in agriculture.

2. Food Tech:

Food technology aims to meet consumer demands for healthier, safer, and more efficient food production while reducing waste and supporting sustainability.( https://www.siridhanyamillet.com/ )

3. Edu-Tech:

Educational technology focuses on meeting diverse learning needs, providing accessible education for all, enhancing teaching and learning experiences, and supporting educational research.

4. Health-Tech:

Health technology innovations aim to improve patient outcomes, reduce healthcare costs, enhance the patient experience, advance medical research, and promote population health.

5. Clean Energy:

Innovations in clean energy help reduce greenhouse gas emissions, decrease dependence on fossil fuels, create jobs, stimulate economic growth, and improve public health.
