Operational Strategy

1. Awareness :

SGIARC-TBI is committed to raising awareness within the community through the organization of impactful awareness camps, where we aim to inform individuals about the existence of our start up incubation centre, as well as the range of comprehensive support we offer, guiding entrepreneurs from the initial stages of ideation to successfully entering the market.

2. Formation of domain specific clubs & associations:

Utilising our robust industry network and esteemed alumni, we are undertaking a strategic initiative to establish domain-specific clubs and associations. This endeavor is designed to offer invaluable support to individuals who are embarking on their entrepreneurial ventures or seeking guidance from technical and domain experts. By creating clubs and associations within relevant domains, our primary goal is to cultivate an environment where seasoned professionals can share their expertise with our incubatees, thereby enhancing their entrepreneurial endeavors.

3. Need identifications:

Our approach involves diligently identifying and understanding the needs of society concerning their problems and ideas. Through comprehensive research and engagement, we proactively assess the challenges faced by individuals, enabling us to gain a deep understanding of their pain points and aspirations. This empathetic approach allows us to tailor our services and support to effectively address the specific requirements of the community, ensuring maximum impact and value creation.

4. Mentoring (workshops/hackathons /dedicated training etc.)


Business mentoring:
Our organization boasts a diverse team of 100+ experienced business advisors who are dedicated to assisting innovators and entrepreneurs in transforming their startups into successful businesses. Our primary objective is to offer invaluable guidance and expert knowledge, equipping entrepreneurs with the necessary tools to overcome challenges, capitalize on opportunities, and achieve long-term, sustainable growth.

Technical support for ideation to product or service:
Through our extensive network and association with various industries/organizations such as alumina, SINE, Venture Centre, Pune, NASSCOM, we offer comprehensive technical support throughout the entire journey, from ideation to the final product or service. Our track record includes successful guidance of more than six startups, which are now self-sustainable and earning in crores. Leveraging this background, we provide entrepreneurs with thorough technical assistance, guiding them from the initial stages of ideation to the development and delivery of their product or service.

IP protection:
Our team possesses extensive expertise in effectively transitioning intellectual property from laboratory settings to global markets, both at the national and international levels.

Financial mentoring:
We have successfully secured funding from multiple agencies and have diligently submitted accurate utilization and expenditure certificates. Through our comprehensive mentoring program, our primary objective is to offer specialized financial mentoring services to individuals, providing them with expert guidance and unwavering support in effectively managing their finances. Our aim is to empower them to make informed decisions and successfully attain their financial goals while navigating the intricacies of financial management.

Goto market strategy:
Our organization offers a meticulously designed and executed plan that is strategically formulated to successfully introduce our product or service to the market, effectively reaching our intended target audience, and ultimately attaining sustainable growth.

Techno legal/legal mentoring:
Our specialized mentoring program provides invaluable techno-legal guidance, bridging the gap between technology and the legal landscape, empowering entrepreneurs to navigate complex legal issues with confidence. Overall, our approach at the incubation center is guided by the aforementioned key points, which include raising awareness through impactful awareness camps, establishing domain-specific clubs and associations, diligently identifying and understanding the needs of society, providing comprehensive mentoring programs, prioritizing intellectual property protection, offering financial mentoring services, developing effective go-to-market strategies, and providing specialized techno-legal guidance. By adhering to these key points, we ensure a comprehensive and well-executed strategy that empowers our incubatees to navigate challenges, seize opportunities, and achieve sustainable growth in their entrepreneurial endeavors.
